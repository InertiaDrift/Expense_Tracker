About the Project

Hello and welcome to my expense tracker! This is my first Django project so it's rather simple and minimialistic but I'm planning on constantly updating this whenever I get time so expect to see big improvements in the future.

Current Features:

  • Effortless Expense Tracking: Easily record your daily expenses and categorize them for a clear overview of your spending habits.
  • Budget Management: Set and monitor budgets to stay in control of your finances.
  • User-Friendly Interface: An intuitive design that makes managing your finances easy and enjoyable.

Upcoming Features:

  • Social Media Login: I'm working on integrating social media login options, making it even more convenient for you to access your account.
  • Expense Overviews: Soon, you'll be able to view comprehensive expense overviews, including trends, recurring expenses, and more.
  • Advanced Analytics: Expect advanced analytics tools to help you make smarter financial decisions.

Please don't hesitate to share any feedback, criticisms or suggestions you might have.
